What Inspired Me

Need Better Solution for Accepting the Technology(IT)

Change is Simple, Understandable, and Predictable but it Needs Time ~Zaheer 

 
It is necessary for us to acknowledge that Pakistan is still a developing country
 and a lot of research is needed to be done in the field of technology and IT,
 in particular, that will help to boost the economy of the country. 
I feel proud to see that the youth of Pakistan is taking an active part in developing 
different kinds of impressive and useful software including health, 
financial, and social applications. Then the question arises is where is the problem? If
 everything is going well and lots of applications are being developed.
 The problem as a Pakistani that I find and feel is that we are not getting ready
 ourselves to accept the technologies maybe because we do not trust 
technologies; we have privacy and usefulness concerns in adopting the technology. 


Lack of education and training to such platforms are the main cause of these
 problems so why do not we try to make software or applications that educate and 
train people while entertaining them normally we call such applications as 
edutainment. I also believe that there are so many advanced innovation ideas that 
I can present through this challenge/platform but my point is to make the base 
of this innovation clear for everyone because the base for all innovations is 
how well we adapt and accept the technology and the famous saying is “But The Base 
Is On What People Know”. We also should know that the Behavioral intention
 of a person or a user is a factor that leads people to use the technology and we can 
train the people even by not letting them know that they are 
under training for something and in that we can be successful to change their behavioral intentions
 related to Perceived usefulness Perceived ease of use,
 Perceived Enjoyment, Security, Social interaction, Attitude, Trust, Privacy, and most importantly
 Intention to use.
I would like to motivate and inspire people to adopt and follow my simple 
philosophy regarding change “Change is Simple, Understandable, and Predictable but it 
Needs Time” so that we can overcome the gap between a user and a technology.
